Potato Chips Ketchup Chips

Experience the timeless and beloved taste of Delina Ketchup Chips. Crafted by the esteemed Lina Industrial Group, this product exemplifies their unwavering commitment to excellence. Delina Ketchup Chips boasts an irresistible flavour and health benefits, thanks to using only the finest potatoes and innovative Opti-flow technology. With each chip perfectly crispy and delicious, Opti-flow technology ensures uniform frying and significantly reduces the production of acrylamide - a harmful substance commonly found in potato chips. Notably, Delina Ketchup Chips contain less acrylamide than the standard level set by the European Union.
