Introduction of Company

LFI food company was established in 2021 in sulaymanieh industrial zone of Kurdistan in order to produce healthy and natural products.

While developing and constructing new production lines and subsidiary companies, this company is currently operating in an area of 16500 square meters. Now, the company produces diverse and healthy products, has more than 120 personnel and 5000-square-meter floor area including shop floor, facilities and warehouses. In addition, 3000-square-meter facility is being built and utilized in order to expand and develop the products of company.

Delina brand is currently registered in most countries of the world and is the first registered famous brand among Kurdish trademark. From the very first, production of healthy products with a special flavors is the motto to which LFI sticks; The Company is trying to always be a progressive and innovative in all products.

Vision

LFI FOOD started its operation in order to create experiences of new unique flavors. Our prospect is to produce a variety of potato produtions using natural and high-quality ingredients. Our products should create your happy and memorable moments.

Mission

Lina Industrial Group believes in not only deliciousness but also quality. Therefore, the company, that has the strongest team, has used the healthiest ingredients and different and exclusive formulas in order to make healthy and delicious products for all of its fans.

Progressive Goals

Lina Industrial Group has considered the next three years as an opportunity to realize short-term goals. Our main goals is to be one of the top five brands in the field of sweets and one of the top two brands in the field of brine pickled. The company tries to stabilize the presence of its products in the markets of the region in the next five years in order to maintain growth and competitiveness on the global level. Furthermore, to establish a prominent and comprehensive presence in the world markets of America and Europe in the next ten years is one of the most important goals of this company. Furthermore, the most important goal of Lina Industrial Group, which has been our first plan from beginning to now, is to honor and respect our dear customers; therefore, one of most important steps taken to realize this goal is to produce one to three new products every year.
